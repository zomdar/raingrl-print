Direct to Garment Printing

What's direct to garment (DTG) printing? It's a speciality printing technology that allows us to print your design right onto the fabric. We're able to print at a much higher precision than screen printing while using as many colors as needed. This perfect for photorealistic and vibrant prints.

Heat Transfer Vinyl

Perfect for sports teams or shirts with a single color design. We use a vinyl cutter that cuts a silhouette on a roll of vinyl. Once that's completed, we heat press the design directly onto the shirt.

Heat transfer vinyl is great for getting team numbers and logos printed on jerseys. Ask us about getting customized names as well!
